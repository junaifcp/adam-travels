About Us

With the vision of progress, Adam Travels and Cargo has been at the forefront in developing and promoting diversity around Oman | UAE | India of travel experiences. Running for almost 11 years as a Omani owned company. Adam tours and travels has grown with its people whose warmth and hospitality remain as its unique asset. We are now established in all around OMAN | UAE | INDIA

Since it opened in 2014 August founded by Faisal Parakkot, we have opened four branches across Oman. The delivery of travel products and services will always be experimental along with the adoption of innovation and technology trends. The “new normal” will not be a bottleneck as Adam refuses to rest on its laurels and continues to re-invent ways in achieving its goal.

11 Years of celebration

On its 11th year, Adam Travel Corporation will remain competitive with the rest of the world in covering the whole spectrum of the travel business and beyond.
